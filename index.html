About Durt Productions

Dotun Oladiti is an Accountant by profession. He is a graduate of the Department of Management and Accounting, Obafemi Awolowo University, Nigeria. He has been involved in music for over a decade as an Instrumentalist, Vocalist, Music Director and Voice Coach.

He has displayed the dexterity and excellence he has to offer musically to a variety of audiences. A profound worship leader and music minister at Vision Builders Christian Centre, Dotun is the convener of the concert, Expressions (a yearly worship event).
